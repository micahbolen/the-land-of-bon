Introduction

My name is Vardalia.  Welcome to the Gateway.  There is much I need to tell you and very little time left, so let's begin.

Long ago, a place called the Land of Bon was discovered in the outer reaches of the universe far from what is now known as Earth.  Sentient beings from across the galaxies would travel there to interact with other beings, but most importantly to seek the wisdom of the noble race of Trees which abounded throughout Bon.  The Trees did not speak directly to other beings, but I was chosen to speak for them.

When the Trees of Bon chose to speak, I was instructed to send for representatives of each race of beings whom were judged by the Trees to be the most receptive among their race to receive the Trees' wisdom.

It has been several millennia since the Trees last spoke to anyone, but they have once again decided to speak and you have been chosen to receive their message.

The Gateway

Of course, you will need to accompany me to the Land of Bon.  In front of you is the Gateway that will take us there.  However, the Gateway cannot simply be passed through.  It requires that you understand some parts of the language of the Trees as I have been taught before it will allow you to pass through.

To begin, I will now present to you a small set of runes created by the Trees to represent their native tongue in a manner in which you will come to understand and, hopefully, grow fond of as I have in my many years of playing.  For, it is in play that we can communicate with the Trees of Bon and they with us.

Ardabelota:


One of two runes which represent the passage of time.  One Ardabelota may be placed on the gateway to mark a single day.

Burdabelota: 


The other rune which also represents the passage of time.  One Burdabelota may be placed on the gateway to mark a single year.
